VidFetch is a free, open-source video downloader powered by yt-dlp — supporting over 1,000 platforms worldwide.

Our Mission

VidFetch was built with a simple mission: to give everyone free, fast, and easy access to download videos from the internet for personal use. We believe that saving a video for offline viewing — a workout tutorial, a cooking recipe, a travel vlog — should not require technical knowledge or paid subscriptions.

We are powered entirely by yt-dlp, the world's most capable open-source video downloader, which supports over 1,000 websites and streaming platforms.

Privacy & Safety

We do not store your download history, track your video URLs, or collect any personal information. Every download session is completely anonymous. No account is required and no cookies are stored beyond your theme preference.

Your privacy is important to us. We never sell data, run ads, or use third-party analytics that track you across the web.

Legal & Fair Use

VidFetch is a tool designed for personal, offline, non-commercial use only. Always ensure you have the right to download a video before doing so. Downloading copyrighted content without permission from the copyright holder may violate their terms of service and applicable laws.

Please respect creators, support their work, and only download content you own or have explicit permission to download.
